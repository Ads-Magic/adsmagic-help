Unlocking Pro Features (Cloud Connection)

  1. In your WordPress Admin, go to Ads Magic > Dashboard.
  2. Click the "Connect Cloud" button.
  3. A popup window will appear asking you to "Sign in with Google".
  4. Select your Google Account to create your Ads Magic profile.

    Note: You may see a generic URL during the login process (supabase.co). This is our secure authentication provider. It is safe to proceed.

  5. Once logged in, the popup will close, and your plugin will automatically refresh to show "Connected".
  6. You can now enable Real-Time Sync and Server-Side Tracking.
